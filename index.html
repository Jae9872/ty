🎯 How to Use This Generator

🚀 Quick Start (Recommended)

  1. Copy Job Description: Find the job posting and copy the complete description
  2. Paste & Generate: Paste it into the "Quick Generator" tab and click generate
  3. Copy to AI: Copy the generated prompt and paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, or your preferred AI
  4. Get JSON: The AI will output a complete custom_data.json file tailored to the job
  5. Save & Generate: Save the JSON and run your resume generator

🔧 Individual Prompts

Use the "Individual Prompts" tab when you want to:

  • Generate only specific sections (like just a profile summary)
  • Fine-tune particular parts of your application
  • Create multiple variations for A/B testing

🎨 British Conversational Tone

All prompts include tone instructions for natural, engaging British English that:

  • Uses contractions and simple language
  • Avoids robotic corporate speak
  • Sounds genuinely enthusiastic but professional
  • Passes ATS while remaining human-readable

The generated JSON will contain exactly what your resume system needs:

  • profile.summary - ATS-optimized summary with your credentials
  • core_competencies - Structured skills matching the role
  • cover_letter - Complete personalized cover letter
